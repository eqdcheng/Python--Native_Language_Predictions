What about New Year in my land ?

Many people go out to celebrate this day. They enjoy to have a good meal with hepar adiposum, oyster, champagne and so on.
They enjoy to go here and there to dance.
Midnight is the hour to kiss everybody and to wish a happy new year.
Everybody throws confetti to other people.
The party has to go on till the morning. Then people go together to have breakfast. They go back home and have a little rest because the first January is the day you have to visit parents.
They are so kind! They cooked for you a marvellous meal with a lot of dishes ! But your head is broken and you don't want anything but your bed. You have to smile and to say thanks a lot. But you're happy to know that many people love you.
