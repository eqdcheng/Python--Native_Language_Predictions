dear brother,

I write you this letter to talk about my life at Lyon. We hadn't seen since the last holiday I'm sorry to write not before but every day I have to work a lot to succes at the university. However, I arrive to spend time to do other things, for example the last week-end, i went to a friend's birthday party. By the time it started, I had helpt her to buy the alcohol because she was not 18 years old. And we stayed at the party until it finished because I had to help her to range her apartment, but the day after I was a little tired because I have drunk more that I would have drunk ( don't say that to mum because she will be shocked).
But whereas, I know to spend a good time, I have to work. The next week I will have to do a math's homework, it's a few difficult, however, as soon as I finish it, I will watch an other movies of Tim Burton. I would watch alice an the wonderland but I had no time.
I have to work, I say you goodby, and I expect to see you soon
