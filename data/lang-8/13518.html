my children

Dec 16, 2008 14:15 children
I have three children. My elder son is 29, my younger son is 25 and my youngest daughter is 21.
I didn’t have good health when I was young, so I was not able to take enough care of my children. However my elder son instead of me took care of his brother and sister.
When he was 8, his sister was a baby. He changed her diapers, gave her a bottle and held her in his arms or carried her on his back to sleep. That’s why, she used to watch TV sitting on his laps until she was big enough.
Now, my elder son works in Hiroshima, my younger son is a doctoral student in Nagoya and my daughter is a university student in Tokyo. Though each of my three children lives in each place far from our house, apparently they stay one another. I hope they continue having a good relationship forever.

Dec 16, 2008 15:14
  • He changed her diapers, gave her a bottle and held her in his arms or carried her on his back to sleep.

  • He changed her diapers, gave her a bottle and held her in his arms or carried her on his back until she fell asleep. (I think this is what you mean, or maybe 'while she slept.')

 
  • That’s why, she used to watch TV sitting on his laps until she was big enough.

  • So, she used to watch TV sitting on his laps until she was big enough to sit on her own.

 
  • Though each of my three children lives in each place far from our house, apparently they stay one another.

  • Though each of my three children lives in a place far from our house, apparently they stay close to one another.

 

Good job. It sounds like you have a nice family.

Thank you for your correction!
