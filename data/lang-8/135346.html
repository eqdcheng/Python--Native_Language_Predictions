A lizard

  • 83
  • 0
  • 0

Mar 27, 2010 16:15
It happend on my second day in Brisbane.

A lizard might be try to come in my room.
But it got between window frames.
It was poor. But I was very surprise!
I haven't seen it before. So I was scared,
I asked for help to my homestay friend,
And she asked our homestay mom.
And then she asked her husband.
After all, my homestay daddy solved it.
He became a super hero.
But the poor lizard died.

"Good bye. I'm sorry~"
