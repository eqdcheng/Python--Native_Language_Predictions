Jul 13, 2010 16:26 Expo
Recently, I graduated from the university. I have plenty of holidays to be used before I can get the job. So, I decided to travel Shanghai Expo in this summmer holiday. That is a wonderful experience, many things that is showed in Expo is very fresh and new. The staff in the expo are friendly ,they are welcome to answer questions. I also meet some new friends in the trip. They have different nationality ,Taiwan ,Korea and Hong Kong. I love the Expo.

Two years after,Korea is going to organize Expo. I am eager to go visit there. Korea ,I am coming to seeing you soon!
