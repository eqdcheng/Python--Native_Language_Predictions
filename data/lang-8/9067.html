Addicted to Useless Information

  •  
  • 242
  • 1
  • 1
  • English 
Nov 24, 2008 11:24
I spent a lot of time online such as watching many videos on YouTube, reading Wikipedia and blogs. Fortunately, it was Sunday, so doing that all day didn’t make any trouble for anyone.

What a waste of time! I really hate that behavior, but I couldn’t stop that. At first, I felt they were interesting, so seeing it seemed to be a bit valuable to me. A few hours after seeing it, I became aware that they were useless information.

I began to suspect that I am a bit addicted to the Internet. I usually have a lot of plan to do, so I don’t have time to be online long time. However, if I don’t have any specific plan like today, I tend to use my computer too much.

I think I need some kind of restriction for stopping using computer.
