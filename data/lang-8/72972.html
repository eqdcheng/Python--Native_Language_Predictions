Dear soulmate

Where are you?
I’m looking for you everywhere
At the university, at home, in the street…why I can’t find you?
Are trying to meet me? Have you ever tried to imagine how am I?
I tried to dream to with you but in my dreams I can’t see your face
How can I that you are really you if I don´t know your face?

Could you know who am I? I hope so..
Please
if you find me, hug me and don’t let me do
I’m sure that I will try to escape, don’t let me do that
Show me a proof that you are you and I’ll stay with you forever.

But until that I will be there waiting

Your soulmate, Rose
